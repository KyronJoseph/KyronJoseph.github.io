The Game Mechanics

Dodging

most soulslike games have a dodge mechanic where the player dodges or dashes in a certain direction sometimes granting the player invunlnerability

Healing

theres also a healing mechanic present which takes time for a player to complete leading to a risk reward type of scenario

Leveling

since these games are rpgs a leveling mechanic is also present however unlike other rpgs the the leveling mechanic is completely optional
