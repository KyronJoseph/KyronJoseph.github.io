What is a Soulslike Rpg?

Soulslike rpg games are mostly games that have role playing elements of standard rpgs while having unforgiving difficulty

Unforgiving Difficulty

The unforgiving nature of soulslike games is what gives them their reputation as these types of games demand alot out of the player

Harsh Reputation

Because of the difficulty of these games soulslikes have a harsh reputation leading some claiming these games exclusive or problematic by nature

Breaking that Harsh Reality

The point of this site is the break the misconceptions people have about these games by offering a perspective on the games combat
