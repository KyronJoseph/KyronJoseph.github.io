What is a Soulslike Rpg?

Soulslike rpg games are games that have role playing elements of standard rpgs and the unforgiving difficulty of souls games these games also share alot of mechanical similarities to the souls games that they are based on making is easy to put these games in a similar category

Harsh Reputation

These games due to their difficult nature has built up a harsh reputation as being hostile to beginners you wouldnt be amiss to find many articles talking about how these games are often too diffcult

Breaking perception

In truth soulslike games arent exactly overly difficult but require the player to use different skills than stadard action games this websites purpose is to show these things
