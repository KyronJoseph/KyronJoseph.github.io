Leveling System

As with all rpg games there is a level up menu where you can spec into specific stats to customize your character

Vitality This stat levels health is important for staying alive longer
Weapon stats these stats usually are requirements to weild certain weapons like leveling Strength or Dexterity
Endurance This stat will increase the stamina bar making it to where you can commit to more actions
Weight if the game has a equipment load system putting points in this stat will increase how much a character can carry how heavey of armor they can wear
Mana Special attacks in these games consume some type of mana bar which can also be upgraded
